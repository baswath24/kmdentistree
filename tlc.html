Tooth alignment corrections

  1. Home
  2. Services
  3. Tooth alignment corrections

Tooth alignment correction is a procedure to reconstruct several types of malocclusion including overbite, underbite, crossbite, and overcrowding. Each of these alignment problems negatively impacts the functionality and cosmetic appearance of the teeth. Why treat misaligned teeth? Many times, malocclusions, or misaligning of the teeth can cause more problems than one may think. Straighter teeth perform chewing, biting and speaking functions more effectively than crooked teeth. In addition, a straight smile boosts confidence, is aesthetically pleasing to look at, and can help stave off a wide variety of dental ailments.
